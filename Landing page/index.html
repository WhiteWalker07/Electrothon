Who we are?

We are a team of first year students from National Institute of Technology Hamirpur who've made this website for a project. 'Book a Beam' will provide you with a sniff of pre-loved books via donation or sharing.
Although we've started this website with the initiative to help college students who are unable to find books in the library and particularly college girls who mostly remain devoid of the privilege of studying in the library overnight due to hosel in timings, we wish to take this little project of ours to far greater realms of social good.
